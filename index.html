Brightside is a pop/rock band based out of Tallahassee, FL that wins fans over through relentless DIY club tours and opening for national acts.

In 2017 they set out to destroy their van, opening for We The Kings on the WTK10 Tour (5 Florida dates) and quickly following up with two headlining club runs (18 dates). In 2018 they joined Mayday Parade on the first leg of the Welcome To Sunnyland Tour (5 dates from Florida to Texas). Immediately after, they hit the road with Cute Is What We Aim For and Makeout (15 dates in the Northeast and Canada). They have also done several one-off shows throughout Florida with Train, Red Jumpsuit Apparatus, Senses Fail, Theory of a Deadman, and more, as well as playing The Vans Warped Tour (Jacksonville).

They hit the ground running in late 2014 with their first EP produced by Lee Dyess (Mayday Parade, From First to Last, Go Radio). The band got back in the studio in 2017 with Zack Odom and Kenneth Mount (Mayday Parade, Cartel, All Time Low) and James Paul Wisner (Dashboard Confessional, Paramore, Further Seems Forever) to cut their sophomore EP â€œHeart Science." Lead single "AEIOU" got regular spins on Tallahassee's Hot 104.9 FM. Brightside is gaining ground on Spotify with over 300k streams.
